Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requires completion of 10th standard with minimum 35% marks. Selection is based on merit and availability of seats in respective branches.

What is the fee structure?

The total fee is approximately ₹29,000 per year, which includes admission fees (₹6,595), government fees (₹13,405), form fees (₹4,000), and college fees (₹5,000).

Are hostel facilities available?

Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities, security, and mess facilities.

What are the placement opportunities?

We have a dedicated placement cell with 95% placement rate. Top companies from various sectors visit our campus for recruitment.
